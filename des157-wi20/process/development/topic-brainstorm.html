DES 157: Brainstorm Process

Session I

Summary

From the in-class exercise, I found myself particularly interested in the topics about respect and reciprocity; I wanted to find a connection between these ideas in relation to global warming. The mindmap method of brainstorming was useful in this regard since it allowed me to take a general topic and narrow it down into different objects of focus. Furthermore, the simplifying of the topic helped me see more connections to the project's overall theme. While the final idea is still fuzzy, some potential topic paths have been discovered thanks to this exercise.

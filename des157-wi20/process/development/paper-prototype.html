Paper Prototypes

This particular mapping exercise was helpful in testing out "interactive" features for my project. The prototypes I created (see below) were relatively simple, but their simplicity helped me discover the explorative nature of certain users. More specifically, the users I tested with were inclined to explore every aspect of the paper "website", searching beyond the highlighted "buttons" created.

The intention of this design is to have a sort of "timeline" or tree root that follows the tragedy of the commons effects.By clicking through, the user can explore different topics about the tragedy of the commons, and the possible solutions to such a crisis. I would like to add more animations in later iterations of this project, but I will have to see how doable of a task that will be.
